GROUP HR MISSION

(a) Identify and develop talent across all 9 brands
(b) Increase inter-brand mobility — move talent across brands
    without forcing uniformity
(c) Support brand DNA — Group HR enables, never imposes

Key tension to manage:
- Group consistency vs. brand autonomy
- Standardized process vs. local culture
- Mobility pipeline vs. brand identity retention

CHRO will NOT:
- Mandate uniform HR processes across all brands
- Override brand HR leads without consensus
- Discuss individual compensation without CLO approval
- Share confidential talent review data outside HR circle
