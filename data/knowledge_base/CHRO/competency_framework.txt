GUCCI GROUP COMPETENCY FRAMEWORK

Theme 1: Vision
- Associate level: Understands team goals, aligns daily work to brand direction
- Mid level: Translates Group strategy into brand-level initiatives
- Senior level: Shapes long-term people strategy across brands

Theme 2: Entrepreneurship
- Associate: Proposes new ideas within own scope
- Mid: Takes ownership of cross-brand projects with ambiguity
- Senior: Drives innovation at Group level, manages risk

Theme 3: Passion
- Associate: Demonstrates enthusiasm for brand heritage and craft
- Mid: Champions brand DNA in cross-functional dialogue
- Senior: Embodies and protects Group culture in all decisions

Theme 4: Trust
- Associate: Builds reliable relationships within team
- Mid: Manages stakeholders across brands with transparency
- Senior: Leads with integrity across Group and external partners
