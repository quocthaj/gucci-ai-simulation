GUCCI GROUP — GROUP DNA

9 brands: Gucci, Bottega Veneta, Saint Laurent, Balenciaga,
Alexander McQueen, Brioni, Boucheron, Pomellato, Qeelin

Group DNA (shared across all brands):
- Creativity rooted in craft
- Commercial excellence without compromising artistry
- Sustainability as a business imperative
- People as competitive advantage

Brand autonomy principle:
- Each brand maintains its own creative director
- Brand HR has operational independence
- Group HR sets framework, brands adapt execution
- No brand is forced to adopt Group initiatives verbatim
